Grammatical Cases

Case Suffix Explanation English prep. Example Translation
Subjective/Nominative Initial form - answers the questions "who?" or "what?". All dictionaries give nouns in the nominative. - The student is reading. Čë ctudict lësä.
Objective/Accusitive -(t)iv Designates the object of an action. - (object, whole) I read the newspaper. Mi lësä čurnaliv.
Indirect object/Dative -tëji Designates that something is given or addressed to the person (object). to I give it to the friend. Mi xëvä tet amiktëji.
Possessive/Genitive -(t)en Used to show that something (somebody) belongs or refers to something (somebody). - (-'s) The bank of the river. Bank čë rijuten.
Instrumental -(t)am Used to denote an instrument that helps to make something. with, using I write with a pencil. Mi črivä ctilam.
Prepositional -sa Used to designate a place, or a person (object) that is an object of speech and thought. of, for, on, under, etc. The flowers are on the table. Čë vluren čtandën po čë tabelsa.
